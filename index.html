Greetings From
Professor Dumbledore!

We all Know Professor Albus Dumbledore the headmaster of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.Dumbledore is self-sacrificing and benevolent, ready to risk his own life to preserve the happiness of others. When Harry and Dumbledore reach what they believe to be Merope’s locket, Dumbledore insists that Harry pour the entire basin of potion down his throat, no matter how much he may protest When Dumbledore is killed by Snape, his funeral is attended by students, teachers, Ministry officials, giants, merpeople, and various other human and nonhuman mourners. The diversity of the crowd speaks to Dumbledore’s welcoming, nonjudgmental nature. He is indeed The Greatest Wizard And The best headmaster of Hogwarts.

HARRY POTTER!

Harry James Potter
The Boy Who Lived .One of the most famous wizards of modern times. The only child and son of James and Lily Potter. The Boy Who Was Destined To Do Great Things.
Harry was the only person to have ever survived an attack by Voldemort—by somehow rebounding the latter’s “killing curse” which left him with a lightning-bolt-shaped scar on his forehead.
Harry facing almost certain death in the struggle against the evil wizard. With help from those who love him and believe in him, he was able to emerge victorious.And Did What no one else could have done

Ron Weasely!

Ron Weasley is one of Harry’s best friends.Ron and Harry meet on the train to Hogwarts and become fast friends. Ron serves as a gateway to help Harry learn about some of the customs of the wizarding world, and alleviates Harry’s fears that he won’t know how to do any magic because he didn’t grow up around it.Ron accompanies Harry on nearly all of his adventures.Ron was a truely loyal friend and a good man
Ron Grows Out to be one of the best Wizards Of Hogwarts

Hermione Granger!!

Muggle or the smartest Wizard In Hogwarts?You Decide. At the outset, she is an annoying perfectionist, a goody-two-shoes who has read all the books for her classes in advance, has learned all about Hogwarts, and never breaks the rules. When she first speaks to Harry on the train ride to school, she is eager to impress him with her knowledge, whereas Harry only wants to make friendswhen Harry and Ron are reprimanded for trying to save her from the monster and she coolly delivers a bold-faced lie to the teacher. The little girl who has been abiding by all the school rules now dares to lie to her superiors, and a new friendship is born .Creating THE LEGENDARY TRIO

The Legendary Trio!

The friendship in Harry, Ron and Hermione[the legendary Trio]. The end of Voldemort demonstrated the tremendous depth of the friendship between the three of them, as Ron and Hermione both vowed to go with Harry wherever his journey may take him. Ron says, “We’re with you whatever happens,” without having to ask Hermione, showing that the three of them have an understanding between them. Hermione’s remark that, “there was time to turn back if we wanted to. We’ve had time, haven’t we?” echoes back to the days, reminding us of everything that these three have faced together

Lord Voldemort!

Voldemort aka Tom Riddle dedicated his life to the Dark Arts, world domination in the form of a wizard-centric pureblooded society, and the pursuit of immortality. The quest for immortality led Voldemort to create Horcruxes, or objects that house parts of his soul, since he cannot truly die while the Horcruxes exist. Though Voldemort always intended to make seven, he incorrectly believes that he only made six—Harry is the unknown final Horcrux, but having his soul split so many ways means that Voldemort's soul is too fragile for him to recognize this. A person must kill in order to make a Horcrux, and Voldemort in his cruelty has no problem with this. He's known as the most evil wizard in history and kills people for any reason, or no reason at all.

Rubeus Hagrid!

Who Doesn't Like seeing Hagrid. Our Favourite giant from Hogwarts .During the first part of the fighting, Hagrid was captured by Voldemort’s giants and tied up in the forest. After Harry was “killed,” Hagrid was forced to carry his friend back to Hogwarts as a symbol of Voldemort’s complete victory. Of course it didn’t turn out quite like Voldemort planned.The Giant was always there for Harry Was there any friend more loyal and true than Rubeus Hagrid? he was always there for Harry. After all, Hagrid was Harry's very first welcome into the wizarding world, and because of his hospitality, Hogwarts truly became Harry's home.
